Luau Tickets

Please fill out this form to purchase a ticket for the Engineering Luau that will be hosted on Saturday, July 13th, at the Beach Waterpark.

This years theme is "Bicentennial" to celebration 200 Successful Boldly Bearcat years! Engineering Luau starts at 11:30 am and goes until 10:30 pm.

The only payment method accepted online is Venmo (@CEAS-Tribunal), if you can not pay through Venmo please reach out to our Luau Chair Ahsan Subzwari at subzwasa@mail.uc.edu.
